About the System

This is a web-based management system designed for domestic branch-to-branch logistics operations inside Cambodia. It provides a centralized platform for tracking shipments as they move between different provincial branches and cities.

The main focus is on transparency and easy tracking for all stakeholders: customers can see where their packages are, drivers can update status on the go, and branch staff can coordinate shipments efficiently. This system was designed and developed as our bachelor final project in Information Technology.
